What is Violin Retirement?

Violin Retirement is a comprehensive financial planning tool designed specifically for individuals pursuing Financial Independence and Early Retirement (FIRE). Our platform combines elegant portfolio tracking with powerful modeling tools to help you chart your course to financial freedom.

Key Features

  • Portfolio Tracking: Monitor your investments across multiple accounts and asset classes in real-time.
  • FIRE Calculator: Project different retirement scenarios and visualize your path to financial independence.
  • Goal Setting: Set savings milestones and track your progress toward early retirement.
  • Withdrawal Planning: Model safe withdrawal rates using proven strategies like the 4% rule.

Privacy & Security

Your financial data is yours alone. We use industry-standard encryption and secure authentication to protect your information. Your portfolio data stays private and secure.

Who is this for?

Whether you're just starting your FIRE journey or years into accumulation, Violin Retirement provides the tools and insights you need to stay on track. Perfect for DIY investors who want deep control over their financial planning.
